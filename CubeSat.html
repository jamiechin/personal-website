CubeSat



Web Design, Visual Design

www.cubesat.org

One of the projects I completed as a Web and Graphic Designer at PolySat was to completely redesign CubeSat's website and make it more accessible, user friendly, and organized.

The CubeSat Program was developed by California Polytechnic State University and Stanford University as a way to assist businesses and universities in flying payloads in space, providing a standard, flight proven deployment system, and is part of the NASA CubeSat Launch Initiative.
At the moment, I am also working on recreating PolySat's website (currently: www.polysat.calpoly.edu) and a dual team website.
