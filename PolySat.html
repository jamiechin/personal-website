I have always held a long love for the ocean and what lies beneath it. I grew up as a competitive swimmer and water polo player, and some of my current hobbies include scuba diving, surfing, and paddle boarding. Naturally, the water is my second home. What I love is reflected in what I create, so it was inevitable for me to reproduce my 2D drawings into a tangible and fun project - playing cards!

Throughout the past year, I have accumulated a number of kraken / octupus sketches. After gathering all of them, I then proceeded to digitize them to prep them for print. This is a complete 52 card deck, and ready to play!








How all beginnings are: several sketches with no general aim.
