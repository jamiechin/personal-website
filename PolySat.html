    For the past year I've been working at PolySat, an organization for students involved in multidisciplinary space technologies and building satellites at Cal Poly.

After finishing CubeSat's website, I have recently finished designing PolySat's website!
















    ISX Mission Logo

    The purpose of the ISX mission is to measure how plasma tubes in the ionosphere burst when full of energy, by waiting until sunset and reading DTV signals from the ground. I wanted to the ISX Logo to reference the plasma tubes in a clean, minamalistic manner.
