hey! i'm jamie.

I often think I'm the luckiest person in the world, with endless opportunities to study what I love - the intersection between design, technology, and the user. Currently at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication, Web and Digital Media Concentration & a Minor in Science, Technology, and Society. Growing up in the San Francisco Bay Area, my long-term affair with the interwebs began at an early age with creating neopets hacks websites and coding tumblr themes. My enthusiasm about design and development is still as fresh and alive to me as it was back then, and I cannot wait to learn more.

I try not to take life too seriously. Doing what makes my soul happy: Being thoughtful, the outdoors, creating cool stuff, reading and traveling often, water sports, and petting all the dogs.
