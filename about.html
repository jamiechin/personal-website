Growing up in the SF Bay Area, I've long been fascinated about visual communication and turning design into solutions to push society forward. My end goal is to use design in a holistic approach to improve the overall quality of life, even if it is only for one individual. My practice is interdisciplinary, spanning design and society; my focuses, skills, and interests are constantly evolving.

Currently, I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication, Web and Digital Media concentration at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo.

One of my favorite pasttimes is designing for space exploration and research. I've worked at CubeSat and SEDS USA - Students for the Exploration and Development of Space. Currently, I'm a intern at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory with the Electronic Publishing team in the Technical Information section.
