hello!

I often think about how I'm the luckiest person in the world to have the opportunities to study what I love: the intersection between design, technology, and the user. Growing up in the San Francisco Bay Area, I was raised immersed in art and technology. My enthusiasm about design and development is continuously thriving while I'm at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Graphic Communication, Web and Digital Media Concentration, emphasis in UI/UX & a Minor in Packaging. I'm fascinated about how design can turn into a tangible, enjoyable, and usable solution to help resolve issues and improve the quality of life.

My end goal is to create something that will leave an impact and last longer than I do. When I'm not designing, I live for the simple things. Being thoughtful, the outdoors, rainy days, traveling, water sports, coffee + a few good books, and petting all the dogs.
