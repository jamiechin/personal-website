100 Days of UI Design



User Interface, Visual

• Sketch
• Adobe Illustrator

This is a personal design challenge where I am creating 100 UI elements every day for 100 days. By putting in an hour or two every day, I hope to learn more about myself, current trends, my process of designing, and prioritization/time management. Some struggles I have already encountered are choosing sleep or staying up to put in hours to become a better designer. As somebody who thrives off a busy schedule, I've come to see that prioritization is foundational. If something is important to me, I will find time for it, and becoming a better designer but having very little free time is a problem that I battle with.
